About us

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

NovaSect (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates novasect.space. This page explains what data is collected when you use the platform, by whom, and how it is used. NovaSect does not charge for access, sell data, or run advertising.

What We Collect

NovaSect itself does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not operate login systems, contact forms, analytics dashboards, or cookies of our own.

Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are loaded as part of this platform. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google’s CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log your IP address and browser headers when serving font files. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • TradingView — interactive charts on Sentinel and report pages are served via TradingView widget embeds. TradingView may set cookies and log usage data. See TradingView Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare CDN (Three.js) — the Three.js library is loaded from Cloudflare’s CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). Cloudflare may log request metadata. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

Market Data Sources

Live data displayed on this platform is sourced from Yahoo Finance, FRED (Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis), Finnhub, and Alpha Vantage via our own serverless proxy endpoints. Your queries are forwarded to these services with no personal identifiers attached beyond standard server request headers (IP address, User-Agent). We do not log or retain these requests.

Vercel Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel’s infrastructure may collect server access logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) for operational purposes. See Vercel Privacy Policy.

Cookies

NovaSect does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party embeds (TradingView, Google Fonts) may set cookies governed by their own policies.

Your Rights (EEA / UK)

Because NovaSect does not collect personal data, there is no data to access, rectify, or erase on our end. For data held by third-party services listed above, please contact those providers directly using the links above.

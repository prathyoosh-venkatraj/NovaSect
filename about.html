About us

Who are we?

NovaSect is an intelligence platform engineered for financial analysis and equity research within the global Energy, Utilities, and Industrials sectors. We bridge the gap between raw data and actionable insight, providing analytics that span the entire corporate spectrum. Our operations consist of the decomposition of both static and dynamic datasets in an effort to equip our users with a granular understanding of the fundamental drivers shaping the global energy, utilities and industrials sectors.

About our founder
